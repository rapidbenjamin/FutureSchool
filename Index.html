Conceived by tech industry executives and forward thinking, experiential teaching professionals, Futurescool was created to inspire young minds to become tomorrow's innovators. Crafted with the assistance of Ivy League alumni's, Futurescools innovative courses presents fun and interactive live after-school classes designed to master personal development, soft skills, digital & financial literacy, current and future technologies and essential sciences to young learners between the ages of 8-18.

Embracing a combination of the Problem based learning framework, the Case Study method pioneered by Harvard and the Moonshot framework at the core of Google's innovation we empower students with a transformative and immersive learning experience necessary for success in education, career and personal growth; shaping them into future innovators of a tech-driven world.
